The most important ingredient of a Tertulia, and the most important ingredient of a chamber music performance, is the intimacy - the physical proximity of our artists and the audience. Not only do world-renowned musicians perform in the intimacy of our host restaurants, they share in the festive and convivial nature of the meal and evening as a whole.

About Tertulia

Tertulia is a chamber music series that is designed to attract new and experienced audiences alike, or really, anyone who loves incredible food and music. The idea is simple: Tertulia presents world-class chamber music concerts in select restaurants around the city, and musicians and audiences share an evening of music, food, drink and conversation.
