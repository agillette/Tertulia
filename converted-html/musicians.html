Tertulia artists join us this season because of a shared love of food, music, and people. One of the main ingredients of a Tertulia is the physical proximity of our artists to the audience. Not only do our world-renowned musicians perform in the intimacy of our host restaurants, they dine alongside the audience and are equal participants in the social aspect of the evening.

About Tertulia

Tertulia is a chamber music series that is designed to attract new and experienced audiences alike, or really, anyone who loves incredible food and music. The idea is simple: Tertulia presents world-class chamber music concerts in select restaurants around the city, and musicians and audiences share an evening of music, food, drink and conversation.
