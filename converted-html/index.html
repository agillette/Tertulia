Bringing together their shared passion for chamber music and New York City's unmatched culinary arts scene, Artistic Directors Julia Villagra and James Austin Smith have created a vibrant concert series aimed at New York's dynamic generation of young music lovers. Tertulia presents concerts in restaurants, an environment that is welcoming and stimulating. At Tertulia, we believe social dining, incredible food, and world-class chamber music, together, are the perfect recipe for engaging today's classical music audiences.

About Tertulia

Tertulia is a chamber music series that is designed to attract new and experienced audiences alike, or really, anyone who loves incredible food and music. The idea is simple: Tertulia presents world-class chamber music concerts in select restaurants around the city, and musicians and audiences share an evening of music, food, drink and conversation.
