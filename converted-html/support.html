Become a Member

Take advantage of special events, open rehearsals, restaurant discounts and more by becoming a member. Specific benefits include:

  • Acknowledgement in every program for our 2014-2015 season
  • A tax-deductible donation to Tertulia, Inc. a 501(c)(3)
  • Special invitations to private events with Tertulia's Board of Directors, Artistic Directors, and Artists
  • Season-long 10% discount at Harding's
  • Priority access to pre-sale tickets
  • Friends and family discounts to select Tertulias
  • Exclusive invitations to open rehearsals

About Tertulia

Tertulia is a chamber music series that is designed to attract new and experienced audiences alike, or really, anyone who loves incredible food and music. The idea is simple: Tertulia presents world-class chamber music concerts in select restaurants around the city, and musicians and audiences share an evening of music, food, drink and conversation.
